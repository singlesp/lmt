Earth Connected

We are all connected by belonging to this Earth. No matter what faith or path you walk, every person benefits from feeling connected to something larger than themselves and to the land beneath them. In every session, I honor natural rhythms, seasonal cycles, and the way our bodies mirror the environment around us.

Body Centered

Your willingness to observe your body is one of the most powerful decisions you can make. The body stores emotions, memories, beliefs, and stories that often surface as pain, tension, or fatigue. I use Thai massage, neuromuscular deep tissue, craniosacral therapy, energy work, and breath support to help you listen and release what no longer serves.

Mindfulness Based

Awareness is the first step toward change. Guided meditation, breath awareness, and body attention all work together to calm the nervous system and create more capacity for healing. During sessions, I may invite you to connect with breath and sensation so your body feels safe enough to soften and reset.
