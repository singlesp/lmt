Meet Miwa

(She/her/hers) Licensed Massage Therapist, SC License #11775 · Certified Thai Massage Practitioner · Craniosacral Therapist

I am a licensed massage therapist, certified Thai massage practitioner, and craniosacral therapist. I come from two generations of healers and psychotherapists, and feel deeply grateful to offer this work.

I see mind, body, and spirit as fully connected. The body holds truth in its posture, patterns, and scars. My gift is working through the physical body as a doorway to deeper healing. Massage is often the gateway, and from there, the therapeutic journey can go as deep as you desire.

I didn't always know I would become a healer. In fact, I resisted it for a long time. But the call was persistent, and once I surrendered to it, everything aligned. I now feel honored to walk alongside my clients on their healing journeys.
