Earth Connected

We are all connected by belonging to this Earth.

No matter what faith or path you walk, every person benefits from feeling connected to something larger than themselves and to the earth. Nature's cycles are a reflection of cycles within ourselves, and my methods and practices are rooted in honoring that connection.

Body Centered

Your willingness to observe your body is one of the most powerful decisions you can make.

Our bodies store emotions, memories, beliefs, and experiences, sometimes manifesting as pain, tension, or discomfort. Through healing touch I help you become more aware of what your body is telling you so you can release what no longer serves you.

I use Thai massage, neuromuscular deep tissue, craniosacral therapy, energy work, and breath to help your body return to balance.

Mindfulness Based

Awareness is the first step towards change.

Guided meditation, breath awareness, and body awareness all go hand in hand. Just 15 minutes of meditation a day has been shown to shift brain and body chemistry. Simple belly breathing can help switch the nervous system from fight-or-flight into rest and repair mode.

During sessions I may ask you to bring attention to your breath and sensations in the body. The breath is the bridge between the conscious and the unconscious, helping your body feel safe enough to let go and heal.
