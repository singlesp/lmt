Meet Miwa

(She/her/hers) Licensed Massage Therapist, SC License #11775 · Certified Thai Massage Practitioner · Craniosacral Therapist

I love what I do and feel incredibly grateful to have landed in a profession that feels like it fulfills and supports me as much as it does my clients. I come from two generations of healers, bodyworkers and psychotherapists, and so really it should not have come as a surprise to me that I landed in the healing arts, and yet I resisted the call for many years. But when I finally began to embrace the call my path began to blossom.

I see the mind, body and spirit as all connected, with there being many paths towards healing and wholeness, but I have found that my gift is to work primarily through the physical, using this as my doorway and entry point to effect change on all levels.

I LOVE using my hands, and I believe the body does not lie. Our bodies are our instrument, uniquely tuned and weathered by all that is you; your life experiences, memories, beliefs, etc. While the mind can play all kinds of tricks on us the body holds and stores the truth, in the form of postural patterns that reflect our beliefs about ourselves, pain and scars that tell the stories of how we got to where we are today, and sensations that are constantly trying to communicate with us if we can just take a moment to listen.

Massage and bodywork is generally my gateway in, but then I find the journey can go as deep as you want it to go.
